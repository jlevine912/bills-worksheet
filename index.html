Welcome to Bills Worksheet 👋
Your personal bill-paying command center. Here's how it works in three easy steps.
1
Add your payees

On the Payees tab, hit + Add Payee for each bill you pay. Start typing a company name and it'll auto-fill the website and category for you. Add the amount you usually pay and whether it's on auto-pay.

2
Pay your bills each month

Hit the ⚡ Track Bills button in the top right. Choose the month, enter your checking account balance, then go through each bill — adjust amounts as needed. Toggle the 💳 switch for anything you're putting on a credit card. This allows you to get points or miles for paying these bills, and still see a tally of the total amount of bills charged to a credit card, so you can make that extra payment amount to your card. Bills marked as Paid with Credit Card will not impact your Remaining Balance.

3
Track it over time

The Reports tab shows a yearly breakdown of every bill, every month — so you can spot trends, compare months, and see exactly where your money goes.

💡 Pro tip: Head to Settings to link this file to your hard drive — your data will save automatically and never get lost. This feature works in Chrome and Edge only.
